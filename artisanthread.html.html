My Story

Welcome to Artisan Thread! My name is Mihiret, and I've been passionate about crochet since I was a little girl learning from my mother in Addis Ababa.

What started as a hobby has grown into a small business where I create beautiful, handmade crochet items using traditional Ethiopian techniques and patterns. Each piece is made with love and attention to detail.

I specialize in working with crochet cloth, incorporating our rich cultural heritage into every stitch. My mission is to share the beauty of Ethiopian craftsmanship with the world while supporting local artisans and sustainable practices.

When you purchase from Artisian, you're not just getting a unique handmade item - you're supporting a small business and helping to preserve traditional Ethiopian crafts.
